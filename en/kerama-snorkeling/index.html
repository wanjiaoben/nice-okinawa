Who can join

  • Ages 3+; even younger children who love water and don't get seasick may join — send height and weight and the captain decides
  • Non-swimmers welcome: life vests worn at all times, staff keep watch
  • Not available if pregnant, or with conditions requiring daily medication (high blood pressure, diabetes); health waiver signed on site
  • No alcohol the night before — smelling of alcohol means no boarding, no refund
  • Fun divers: send cert card photo, last dive date/place, and logged dives

Bring: swimwear (rash guard recommended), towel, drinks, motion-sickness pills. Approximate prescription masks available; contacts work with a normal mask.

FAQ

I can't swim — can I snorkel?

Yes. Life vests stay on the whole time and staff supervise and photograph everyone. A private 1-on-1 guide can be added.

What ages can join?

3 and up; even younger kids who love water may join — send height/weight for the captain to decide. Minors need a guardian along.

Can I choose Nagannu Island?

No — the captain picks the spot on the day, safety first.

What if the weather turns bad?

We confirm around 18:00 the day before. If the trip can't sail, you choose: free reschedule or full refund.

Can I dive without a licence?

Fun dives are certified-only. Non-certified guests snorkel, or ask about introductory diving.

How do I pay?

Payment instructions come with your booking; once details and payment are in, we email your confirmation.
